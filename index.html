Meet RoachNet

An offline-first suite for AI, archiving, and development that actually lives on your machines.

RoachNet treats your computer like a computer again. Models, tools, docs, maps, and your own archive run locally, sync on your terms, and do not disappear because a hosted product changed its roadmap.

A machine that’s yours

Instead of asking which service holds this, you know where it is.

It is in RoachNet. That makes backup, travel, outages, and rebuilds far less stupid.

The internet can still help. It just stops being the floor your work stands on.

RoachClaw

Spin up local AI and keep the heavier work close.

RoachClaw keeps local models on your hardware first, with cloud help only when you actually want it.

No surprise upgrades. No forced migrations. No hosted tab between you and your own machine.
