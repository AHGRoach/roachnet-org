Meet RoachNet

Meet RoachNet. An offline-first suite for AI, archiving, maps, and development that actually lives on your machines.

RoachNet treats your computer like a computer again. Models, tools, docs, maps, and your own archive run locally, sync on your terms, and do not vanish because a login expired or a product manager got bored.

Instead of asking which service holds this, you always know: it is in RoachNet. That makes travel, rebuilds, outages, and backups much less stupid.

Spin up local AI with RoachClaw. Keep a durable archive of the web, docs, and projects you care about. Ship code from a reproducible dev lane that stays the same on the desktop, the laptop, or the lab machine.

No surprise upgrades. No forced migrations. No “we’ve updated our terms” emails between you and your work.
