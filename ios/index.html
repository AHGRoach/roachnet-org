Pairing

Point the phone at your Mac.

The desktop still owns the models, vault, installs, and RoachTail host. The phone stays quick and useful.

Companion URL

http://<your-mac-ip>:38111. Find your Mac’s local IP in System Settings → Wi-Fi → Details.

Pair code

Scan the RoachTail QR or type the join code. Either way you get a signed peer token for this device.

Fallback

Bridge goes offline? Cached chat, vault reads, and runtime snapshots hold until the Mac comes back online.
